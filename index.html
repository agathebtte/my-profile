Animals

Travelling

I love to travel, especially to sunny countries. I find that it allows me to escape from everyday life, to meet new people, to discover new cultures, new languages, new landscapes... I have already been to London, Prague, Berlin, the Canary Islands, Mykonos, the Balearics... But above all, I did a university exchange at the age of 15 in Los Angeles. Indeed, I went alone with my best friend for 3 weeks in a campus to improve my English, but also to visit the city. My next destination is Mexico: I'm going to Cancun in August with my family.

Friends

I spend a lot of time with my friends, I love going on holiday with them, doing activities, going for a drink. I had a great time meeting new people at ESSEC, which helped me to adapt to my new environment. Indeed, I think that the people around us play a key role in our development and happiness.
